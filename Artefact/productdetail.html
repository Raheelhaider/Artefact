Smart Fan

The increase of temperature within a room can be uncomfortable for people, but when the temperature exceeds 40 degrees Celsius, there can be a risk of hyperthermia. To prevent this, our temperature sensor sends a signal to the micro:bit, which then turns on the fan.

Remote/Internet Activated Lights

LED lights such as this can be controlled via the Internet. It is possible to turn these LED lights On/Off from anywhere. This function is accessible through the webpage using the IoT:bit: Internet WIFI extension board for micro:bit.

Auto Windows

Noise pollution above 70dB is adversely affects our health. To combat we design smart windows and doors using micro:bit. When the value of noise exceeds 70dB, the micro:bit will automatically close them.

Work in Progress

Water Level Alarming

Too much water will result in flooding. When the water level rises above the security line, the micro:bit will receive signals and command the lift pump to pump. Relay will drive and supply electricity to raise pump for redundant water pumping when micro:bit receives signal from soil moisture sensor.
